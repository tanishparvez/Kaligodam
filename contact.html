Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your operating hours?

Most of our outlets are open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Specific hours may vary by location.

Do you accept bulk orders?

Yes! We specialize in bulk orders for weddings, corporate events, and festivals. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance for large orders.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we deliver through Swiggy, Zomato, and Amazon. You can also place direct orders by calling our outlets.

Can I customize gift hampers?

Absolutely! We offer personalized gift hampers for all occasions. Contact our team to discuss your requirements.

Are your products preservative-free?

Yes, we use traditional methods and natural ingredients without any artificial preservatives, maintaining the same quality standards since 1928.
