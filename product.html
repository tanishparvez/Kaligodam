Our Varieties Product

From traditional Besan Laddu to premium dry fruit varieties, explore our carefully crafted collection of authentic Indian sweets.

Bhujia with boondiya

Crispy, spiced strands of besan and moth flour, fried to perfection for that signature crunch — the perfect anytime snack.

Desi Special mota boondiya

Soft, juicy pearls of besan soaked in light sugar syrup — a nostalgic sweet from Kaligodam that melts in your mouth and hearts alike.

Kaju barfi

Classic diamond-shaped cashew delicacy made from premium kaju - and pure sugar syrup, offering a smooth, rich, and melt-in-mouth experience.

Kesariya Peda

Soft, rich milk peda infused with pure saffron strands for a royal touch of aroma, color, and taste.

Mahin laddoo

Traditional handcrafted laddoos made with pure desi ghee, roasted gram flour, and a hint of cardamom — rich, aromatic, and melt-in-mouth, just like home.

Rajasthani Peda

Traditional mawa peda from the royal kitchens of Rajasthan — soft, rich, and perfectly sweetened with a hint of cardamom.
