Where it all began

A Simple Belief, An Enduring Legacy

In 1928, in the heart of Kolkata's bustling Burrabazar, a small sweet shop was born with a philosophy that would define it for nearly a century: good taste never goes out of style.

That's how Kaligodam began — not just as a sweet shop, but as a promise. A promise of authenticity, purity, and the unmistakable taste of home that transcends generations.

For nearly a century, Kaligodam has been synonymous with authentic Indian sweets, snacks, prasad, and bhog — each crafted with the same love, purity, and precision that defined our early days.

The Jajodia Family Legacy

Today, as the fourth generation of the Jajodia family carries the legacy forward, Kaligodam continues to blend heritage recipes with modern hygiene and packaging standards, ensuring that every product reaches you as fresh as it was made.

Four generations of master craftsmen have passed down not just recipes, but values — the unwavering commitment to purity, the insistence on authenticity, and the belief that every sweet tells a story of love and tradition.

What Truly Matters

Purity, trust, and tradition — the three pillars that have guided us for nearly a century.

Purity

Every ingredient is carefully selected for its purity. From the finest ghee to premium nuts and authentic spices — we never compromise on quality, just as our founders intended in 1928.

Trust

Built over 96 years, one customer at a time. Trust is earned through consistency, transparency, and the unwavering commitment to deliver exactly what we promise — every single time.

Tradition

Our recipes are treasures passed through four generations. While we embrace modern standards, our methods remain rooted in time-honored traditions that create unparalleled taste.
