Master Craftsman at Work

A Century of Sweet Traditions

Founded in 1928 by Pandit Kali Gopal Das, Kaligodam began as a small sweet shop with a simple mission: to create the finest traditional Indian sweets using authentic recipes and the purest ingredients.

Today, four generations later, we continue to honor our founder's vision. Every sweet, every snack, every delicacy is crafted with the same love, care, and attention to detail that has made Kaligodam a household name for nearly 100 years.

Our Heritage Collection

Discover our carefully curated selection of traditional sweets and savory snacks, each crafted using time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients.

Mahin laddoo

Traditional handcrafted laddoos made with pure desi ghee, roasted gram flour, and a hint of cardamom — rich, aromatic, and melt-in-mouth, just like home.

Kesariya Peda

Soft, rich milk peda infused with pure saffron strands for a royal touch of aroma, color, and taste.

Bhujia with boondiya

Crispy, spiced strands of besan and moth flour, fried to perfection for that signature crunch — the perfect anytime snack.

Kaju barfi

Classic diamond-shaped cashew delicacy made from premium kaju and pure sugar syrup, offering a smooth, rich, and melt-in-mouth experience.
